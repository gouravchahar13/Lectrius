# Lectrius: EV Charging Recommendation System

Welcome to Lectrius, the cutting-edge Electric Vehicle (EV) charging recommendation system designed to make your EV charging experience seamless and efficient. Our system leverages advanced algorithms to recommend the most suitable charging stations based on your location, charging speed preferences, and other personalized criteria.

## Features

- **Personalized Recommendations:** Get tailored charging station suggestions based on your EV's model, your current battery level, and your destination.
- **Real-Time Availability:** Access up-to-date information on the availability of charging stations to ensure you find a spot without the wait.
- **Charging Speed Filter:** Choose charging stations based on the speed you need - from rapid chargers to standard ones.
- **Cost Estimation:** Receive an estimated cost for charging at recommended stations, helping you manage your charging expenses better.
- **User Reviews and Ratings:** Make informed decisions with insights from reviews and ratings provided by other EV owners.
- **Route Integration:** Seamlessly integrate recommended charging stops into your route with our smart navigation system.

## Getting Started

To start using Lectrius, follow these simple steps:

1. **Acess Lectrius WebApp:** Available through any browsing platform, Lectrius is accessible on your desktop, laptop, or mobile device. Simply visit [www.lectrius.com](https://www.lectrius.com) to get started.
2. **Start Receiving Recommendations:** Enter your destination, and let Lectrius guide you to the best charging stations on your route.
